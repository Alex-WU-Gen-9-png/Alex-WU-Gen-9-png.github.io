第二节：朗读短文

Text:
Earth Day was created to draw attention to protecting our common home. As is known to all, the earth keeps suffering from pollution, abuse of resources and even wars. As a result, it is not the beautiful planet as it used to be! Therefore, something must be done to protect the earth. To begin with, we should live a low-carbon life with less consumption of resources. After all, the earth can satisfy our need but not our greed! In addition, we should share and reuse things like clothing, furniture, books and etc. Finally, awareness and technology should be strengthened to boost recycling. In a word, every resident as well as government should lend a helping hand to make the earth a healthy and sustainable planet.

第三节：人机问答

情景提示：Michael的父母和他约定好毕业后他要搬出去独自生活。这让Michael感到焦虑和烦躁。
Q1: What was the agreement between Michael and his parents?
A1: They made an agreement that after graduating, Michael would move out and live alone.
Q2: How did the agreement affect Michael?
A2: This agreement made Michael anxious and annoyed.

第四节：话题表述

A Possible Version:
In recent times, there has been a noticeable trend among students who are increasingly unwilling or even unable to engage in laborious activities. Recognizing the importance of raising the awareness of diligence, our school organized a “Campus Cleanup” event last Friday, aiming to cultivate a stronger work ethic among students.
Kicked off at 9:00 in the morning, we embarked on sweeping pathways, uprooting weeds, and collecting litter around the campus. In the afternoon, we extended the effort to classrooms, the cafeteria, and the reading room.
This event aimed not only at tidying our surroundings but improving our life skills and a sense of responsibility. It stressed the importance of keeping a clean and organized environment for us students.
Importantly, the experience served as a significant lesson that a fulfilling life comes from hard work, not daydreaming. It urged us to cherish the contributions of those diligent individuals who made our society a better place.

    B:  Thanks a lot.

    B:  Yes, What’s the special here?
  4. A:  Salad.
    B:  OK. I’d like two sandwiches, a cup of juice and a salad.

第六节：看图说话

A Possible Version:
Last summer vacation, as I was eating watermelon upstairs, an accidental drop of the peel made a passer-by slipped and fell. Recognizing my mistake, I dashed down the stairs, helped him to stand to his feet and offered a sincere apology. Displaying great understanding, the passer-by, Jack, forgave me. What’s more, I invited him to get upstairs and enjoy the watermelon with us. This unexpected encounter paved the way for our friendship, and we became fast friends throughout the rest of the summer.
